## 2. DeepSeek V4 open-source model matches frontier benchmarks at a fraction of the cost

**DeepSeek · April 24 · Today**

DeepSeek released V4-Pro (1.6T parameters, 49B active) and V4-Flash (284B parameters, 13B active) as open-source previews on Hugging Face. V4-Pro scores 80.6% on SWE-bench Verified — within 0.2 points of Claude Opus 4.6 — at $3.48/million output tokens, versus roughly $25 for Claude. The model uses native 1M-token context and tight Huawei chip integration, cutting inference FLOPs by 73% over DeepSeek-V3.2.

*Why it matters:* Commodity-priced, open-weight models at frontier-grade coding performance compress margins for every closed-model provider and raise the floor for what enterprises should expect to pay.
