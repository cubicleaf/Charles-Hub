# Briefs

Daily morning brief — concise summaries of major tech, AI, and business news. Generated each morning at 6:30 AM Eastern.

Files are named by date (`YYYY-MM-DD.md`) and use a YAML frontmatter block at the top for metadata (date, day of week, story count, sources). The viewer renders these with a literary newspaper layout: DM Serif Display headlines, Roman numeral "At a glance" list, terracotta accents.
